Game Instrutions

 The game takes place on a board with two sets of aligned central holes and two storages, the larger holes. Each player controls the holes on their side as well as the warehouse on their right. The pieces are called seeds.

Start Game

The warehouses are empty (no seeds) and the remaining holes have the same number of seeds.

How to play

Players sow their seeds alternately. In turn, they sow from one of their holes depending on the situation of the last seed sown, they can play again or keep some of the opponent's seeds. The game ends when one of the players runs out of seeds.

Start sowing

Choose a hole on your side (other than the warehouse) and collect all N seeds from that hole. Place 1 seed in the next N wells. Seeding is done counterclockwise. If the next hole is your storage, sow seeds left over as well, continue on the opponent's side If you reach the opponent's storage, do not sow but then continue on your side, if you still have seeds. Play again if the last seed is sown in your own store. Sowing now empty cavity, the last seed falls into the warehouse and returns to play. If the last seed lands in your empty hole, transfer the seeds to your storage.

End Game

Game ends when one of the players cannot play because all their holes are empty. The other player collects seeds in their holes. The player whose warehouse has the most seeds wins.
